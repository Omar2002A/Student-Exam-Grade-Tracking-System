About ExamTrack

ExamTrack helps teachers manage students and exams, and allows students to take exams and track their results.

Our Purpose

ExamTrack provides a simple system for managing online exams, grades, and student performance. We believe that technology should empower educators and students by removing technical barriers to academic success.

How It Works

1. Login

Login as a Teacher or Student to access your personalized dashboard.

2. Manage or Take

Teachers manage exams while students take them in a secure environment.

3. Results

Instantly view, save, and track grades and academic progress over time.
